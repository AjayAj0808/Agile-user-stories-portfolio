📌 Project: Vehicle Maintenance System

## User Story
As a vehicle owner, I want to receive maintenance reminders based on mileage and time so that I can keep my vehicle in good condition and avoid unexpected issues.

## Acceptance Criteria
- User can enter vehicle details and current mileage
- User can set service intervals based on mileage or date
- System sends reminders when a maintenance threshold is reached
- User can mark a maintenance activity as completed
- System automatically updates the next due reminder after service completion

## Business Value
This feature improves vehicle upkeep, reduces the risk of breakdowns, and helps users manage service schedules proactively.

## Priority
High

## Stakeholders
- Vehicle Owner
- Product Owner
- Development Team
- QA Team

## Assumptions
- User enters correct mileage and service history
- Reminder rules are configured in the system
- Notifications are delivered through a supported channel
